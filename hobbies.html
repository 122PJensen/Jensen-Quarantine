Hobbies and stuff

Here is what I do to fight against the boredom of being stuck away from friends.

Basketball

I spend a bit of my time outside practicing my dribbling and shooting in my drivewa with my basketball hoop. I get all the extra time to improve to play next year.
Everyday when I work, I practice my singing to improve. Every wednesday evening, I go downstairs where it's quiet so i can practice singing with my music teacher.
A lot of my time goes into gaming. As I don't leave the house much during the week, I spent time to interest myself in different games I never believed I would play. These consist of games like SCP: Secret Lab, Among Us, and Valorant.
Every weekend my father and I go to the beach that allows fishing. We have a permit to pull up there and fish. It's soothing to just sit and relax as we listen to our radio while we fish.
Usually after I finish my work, I just relax and chill outside and drink some orange cream soda. I would play something on my speaker as I enjoy the nice weather we have been having.
As this quarantine has been going on, I took an interest in longboarding. My father's friend Adam has decided to help teach me how to start off. It looks hard but they say it's fairly easy.
